About This Role

Supporting Federal Personnel Initiatives at Scale

We're seeking a highly motivated Senior Analyst with over five years of experience in human resources, Federal personnel processes, staff alignments, developing position descriptions, and strategic communications. This is a high-visibility role ideal for candidates who are passionate about supporting Federal personnel initiatives and driving organizational effectiveness.

You'll work directly with HR specialists, agency leadership, and subject-matter experts to lead multiple high-priority projects in an evolving organizational environment — shaping how our government customers manage their most important asset: their people.

Key Responsibilities

What You'll Do

  • Lead multiple high-priority projects in an evolving organizational environment
  • Develop, evaluate, and update position descriptions for Federal compliance and alignment
  • Coordinate with HR specialists, leadership, and SMEs on strategic goals and operational effectiveness
  • Develop, edit, and proofread communications — policies, reports, emails, newsletters, presentations
  • Support strategic deliverables aligned with organizational goals
  • Manage content and communications through SharePoint or other CMS platforms
  • Contribute to strategic planning, develop metrics for success, and support performance management
Qualifications

What You Bring

  • 5+ years in Federal HR, personnel processes, staff alignments, and strategic communications
  • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects and adapt to changing needs
  • Experience managing mission-oriented programs and high-priority projects
  • Strong analytical skills — performance management, data mining, statistical analysis
  • Exceptional written and verbal communication
  • Knowledge of organizational change management and operational efficiency
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite and SharePoint
  • Must possess or be able to obtain a government security clearance
Preferred Skills
  • Experience with Federal personnel processes, staff alignments, and developing position descriptions
  • Experience writing and editing business communications — reports, newsletters, presentations
  • Familiarity with communication best practices in corporate or project-based environments
  • Experience with business process development, project management tools, and methodologies
  • Previous experience with SharePoint or other document management systems
